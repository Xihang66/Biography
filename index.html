Xihang Yuan (袁西航)

Xihang Yuan finished his undergradute and got the bachelor degree in mathematics from School of Science at Southwest Petroleum University(SWPU), Chengdu, China, in June 2015. Then he started his graduate study under his supervisor Xiangjun Xie at Southwest Petroleum University, and he received his master degree in mathmatics in 2018. He worked as a mathematics teacher to teach undergraduates Advanced Mathematics, Linear Algebra, Probability and Mathematical Statistics, etc. at Southwest Petroleum University since July, 2018.
